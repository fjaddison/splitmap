Splitmap is a community crafted Blitmap derivative that is completely on-chain.


- You can combine four different original Blitmaps in order to make a Splitmap. One corner, and one color from each composition come together to create a new unique piece of pixel art.
- Splitmaps cost 0.05 ETH to mint.


Every original Blitmap can be used in each four corners of a Splitmap up to sixteen times. Each corner must use a different original.


Join us! Splitmap has zero roadmap, zero utility, and zero promises.
